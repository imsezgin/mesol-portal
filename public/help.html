How to use the portal

  • Go to Practice and pick an exercise from your week
  • Read the instruction carefully, then answer all questions
  • Tap Check Answers to see your score
  • Your results are saved automatically

Exercise types

  • Multiple choice — tap the correct answer
  • Gap fill — type the missing word or number
  • Vocabulary match — match words to meanings
  • MESOL combo — English + Maths together

Forgotten your PIN?

Ask your teacher or the school office. They can reset it for you.

Streaks

Complete at least one exercise per day to build your streak. Your longest streak is shown on the Progress page.
